The Target Market for eXtended Reality (XR)

Because eXtended Reality combines the features of Augmented Reality, Mixed Reality, and Virtual Reality, only a few developers have adopted the usage of this new technology.

The Entertainment Section

A few in the entertainment section have used this technology as it contains certain features of VR with some differences between the hardware and the software itself. In a similar vein to the AR, MR, and VR technologies, the entertainment industry uses it in a way that is near-distinguishable between the three types of virtual simulation.

The Workforce Section

Some companies and workforces have adopted this usage of technology in a similar vein to the AR, MR, and VR technologies in a way that is near-distinguishable between the three types of virtual simulation.
